Common exercises

Listed below are our top workouts that we reccomend to our clients. Through these workouts, our clients can achive the physique they dream of.

Burpee
To preform a burpee, you must first begin in a squat position. Then you must jump in the air, once you land, you must quicly fall to your stomach and do a push-up. Once completing the push-up, you must repeat the cycle.
Plank
A plank is a great ab workout. To preform a plank, place both forearms on the floor and raise your core into the air. Remain holding yourself for a short period of time
Mountan Climbers
Mountain Climbvers ensure that your core is stronger when leaving our facility. To preform mountain climbers, put yourself in a pushup position. Then begin to lift your hips in the air. You will lastly begin moving your legs foward and backwards as if you were climbing a mountain.
